about me

Hello, my name is Zachary and I am a graphic designer and web developer from the United States. My design style can be considered abstract as I like to experiment with shapes, color, and texture. When it comes to web design, I prefer simplicity and accessibility. Websites should serve a functional purpose and design shouldn't trump that purpose.

graphic design

I've been making graphic art for close to a decade. In this time, I've evolved my style to include complex texturing, a large gamut of colors, and an abstract composition. I use Adobe Photoshop exclusively for my art. However, I am skilled at using Illustrator, After Effects, and Blender.
